What we handle

Everything between an idea and a real, registered business.

Setting up a company in Pakistan involves SECP, FBR, banks, and a stack of forms most founders have never seen. Our team takes the whole thing off your plate.

📝

Company registration

SECP filings, MoA & AoA, partnership deeds — whichever entity structure fits, we draft and file it for you.

🧾

Tax registration

NTN with FBR, sales tax registration where applicable. We handle the IRIS portal so you never have to.

🏦

Bank account opening

We help you choose the right bank, prepare the board resolution, and walk you through the account application end-to-end.

📊

Bookkeeping foundation

The day you're registered, you get a working chart of accounts, opening balances, and your first invoices ready to send.

💬

Free advisory

Three months of unlimited check-ins with a senior advisor. Got a question about a contract, an invoice, or a tax notice? Just ask.

📈

Investor-ready financials

If you're raising, we'll help you put together cap tables, runway, burn, and the financial shape investors expect to see.
