Questions, answered

Frequently asked.

If it's all free, what's the catch?+

There's no catch. We make our money on long-term bookkeeping, tax filing, and advisory work — for clients who stay with us after the first three months. Setup is free because we'd rather prove ourselves than charge you upfront. If you're happy after the free period, you stay. If not, you don't owe us anything.

How long does SECP registration take?+

Typically 7–10 business days from the moment we have all your documents. Name reservation takes 1–2 days, and the incorporation itself takes another 5–8 days once SECP processes the e-Services submission. We keep you updated through every step in your portal.

What documents do I need to provide?+

CNIC copies for all directors, address proof, and a few signed forms we'll prepare for you. We'll send you a clear checklist after your first call so nothing's a surprise.

Who is "we"? Who's actually doing the work?+

Curator is a service from NorthNext Ventures, run by a team of Chartered Accountants based in Pakistan. We use the same software we build for our larger clients — including NIMBY, our accounting platform.

What happens after the free three months?+

If you'd like us to keep handling your bookkeeping, tax filings, and compliance, we'll propose a monthly fee scaled to your business size. Most clients pay between Rs. 15,000–60,000 per month. You're never locked in — month-to-month, no contracts.

I already have a business. Can you take it over?+

Yes. We do takeovers from other accountants regularly. The first call covers what's already in place, what's missing, and what needs cleaning up. We'll be honest about scope.

Is it really only for Pakistani businesses?+

For now, yes. SECP and FBR are Pakistan-specific, and we want to do them properly rather than half-heartedly. If you're abroad with Pakistani operations, get in touch — we'll let you know if we can help.
